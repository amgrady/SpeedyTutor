About Us

SpeedyTutor is an interactive mobile app that connects students to tutors within seconds. We specialize in many areas to help students succeed in the classroom. Our app not only acts as a platform to improve students learning and education, but also holds the potentional for our tutors to create full-time jobs by monetizing their knowledge and sharing it with the community.

Students

SpeedyTutor locates every tutor in your area that teaches the subject you need within seconds. Then you simply choose the tutor they would like to work with and start scheduling sessions at any time.

Students:

  1. Create a profile using Facebook or Google
  2. Select the subject you need help with
  3. Select your tutor
  4. Add payment options
  5. Start studying!

Tutors

After you sign up through our mobile app, you will be seen by every student in your area. Students can then see your credentials and begin selecting who they want tot work with. The best part? You set your own schedule!

Tutors:

  1. Create a profile using Facebook or Google
  2. Attach a file of your background check
  3. Select the subjects you wish to tutor
  4. Add bank account information
  5. Start studying!

Student FAQ

How do I create an account with the app?

In the app, download the app from the Apple Store or Play Store. Register as a student. Start working within minutes.

Can I work with more than one tutor?

Absolutely. You can work with any tutor based on your liking.

Does SpeedyTutor offer tutoring for homeschool students?

SpeedyTutor offers homeschooling services, barring a background check request by the tutor itself or someone who requested it for the tutor. Please note that this is not a requirement by SpeedyTutor. Background checks can be ordered with our partner Checkr.com

How do I pay my tutor for a lesson?

Once you request for the session, it will hold the funds. After the session, the tutor will collect the payment. SpeedyTutor only accepts credit and debit card; tutors cannot be paid with cash.
